The Future of Personalized Skincare

RemedySense addresses the challenge of finding effective, natural skincare by providing data-driven, personalized recommendations.

INPUT

1. Define Your Profile

Start by providing key information about your skin, including type (e.g., oily, dry), age group, and specific concerns you're facing, such as acne, redness, or dark spots.

PROCESS

2. Predictive Analysis with Decision Trees

The system uses a Decision Tree Classifier trained on clinical efficacy data. This model analyzes the user's skin profile to predict the most effective combination of anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties needed for the specific concern.

OUTPUT

3. Receive Your Remedy

Get a ranked list of natural remedies tailored specifically to you, complete with instructions on preparation and application for optimal results.
